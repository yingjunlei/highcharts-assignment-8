Source: National Center for Education Statistics, Table 303.70.

This data describes the rate of total undergraduate fall enrollment in degree-granting postsecondary institutions from 1980 to 1985. Both full-time and part-time are included and separated by the sex of student.Starting in 1980, 2-year institutions include schools accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges of Technology.

Source: United Nations Population Division, World Population Prospects: The 2017 Revision

This chart describes the population change in percentage from 1955 to 2020 (projected) of Somalia and Afghanistan. This 2017 Revision of World Population Prospects represents the most recent global set of demographic projections prepared by the Population Division of the Department of Economic and Social Affairs of the United Nations Secretariat. The sudden dip in population in 1985 in Somalia can be explained by Somali’s dispute with Ethiopia; Somalia dissidents with Ethiopian air support invaded Somalia in the center, threatening to split the country in two. In the case of Afghanistan, the Soviet–Afghan War plagued the country from 1979 to 1989.
